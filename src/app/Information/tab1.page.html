Why we concern only concern about carbohydrate and fat? The ketogenic diet is a very low-carb, high-fat diet that shares many similarities with the Atkins and low-carb diets. It involves drastically reducing carbohydrate intake and replacing it with fat.

How to perform manual conversion on the food not part of the dropdown list? Please first convert your food to 1 gram, and then value how 1 gram of this type of food would generate the amount of carbohydrate and fat in terms of grams based on proportion. For instance, if 10 grams of such food generates 5.2 grams of carbohydrate. Then 1 gram of the food would generate 0.52 grams of carbohydrate. To determine the proportion, you need to divide 10 by the total food per 10g. 10g รท total food per 10g = 1 g food choice. Then total carbohydrate per 5.2g รท total food per 10g = 0.52 g. Following such method would get you the desired result.
